About NUMOD

NUMOD is an international open-access academic journal focused on publishing high-quality research articles across multiple disciplines. The journal promotes knowledge sharing, innovation, and scholarly excellence through freely accessible research publications, supported by a rigorous double-blind peer review process and a fast, transparent editorial workflow.
